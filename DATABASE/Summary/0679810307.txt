Title: Shabanu: Daughter of the Wind (Border Trilogy)
Author: SUZANNE FISHER STAPLES
Category: Fiction

Summary:
Shabanu: Daughter of the Wind is the first book in the Border Trilogy series by Suzanne Fisher Staples. It follows the story of Shabanu, a young girl living in the Cholistan Desert of Pakistan, who is thrust into adulthood when she is promised in marriage to an older man. Shabanu must navigate the traditions and expectations of her culture while also facing the challenges of her new life. This coming-of-age tale explores themes of family, love, and the struggle for independence in a male-dominated society.
