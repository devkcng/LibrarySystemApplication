Title: The Dragons of Eden: Speculations on the Evolution of Human Intelligence
Author: Carl Sagan
Category: Science

Summary:
In this thought-provoking book, renowned scientist Carl Sagan explores the origins of human intelligence and its role in the evolution of our species. He delves into topics such as the development of the brain, language, and consciousness, and offers compelling speculations on how these factors have shaped human history and our place in the universe. Through accessible language and captivating examples, Sagan sheds light on the mysteries of human intelligence and its connection to the natural world.
