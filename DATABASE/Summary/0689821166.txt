Title: Flood : Mississippi 1927
Author: Kathleen Duey
Category: History

Summary:
In "Flood: Mississippi 1927," Kathleen Duey tells the story of the devastating flood that occurred in the Mississippi River in 1927. The book explores the causes and effects of the flood, as well as the experiences of those who were impacted by it. Through vivid descriptions and personal accounts, readers gain a deeper understanding of one of the worst natural disasters in American history.
