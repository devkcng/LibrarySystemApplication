Title: OUT OF THE SILENT PLANET
Author: C.S. Lewis
Category: Fiction/Science Fiction

Summary:
Out of the Silent Planet is a science fiction novel by C.S. Lewis that follows the journey of a man named Dr. Elwin Ransom to the planet Malacandra. Ransom is kidnapped by two men and taken to Malacandra, where he discovers a peaceful and intelligent alien species. As he learns about their culture and society, he also uncovers a sinister plot by humans to exploit Malacandra for their own gain. With the help of his new alien friends, Ransom must find a way to stop the humans and protect the planet before it's too late. Along the way, he also learns valuable lessons about humanity and the importance of respecting other worlds and their inhabitants.
