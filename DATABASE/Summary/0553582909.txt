ISBN: 0553582909
Title: Icebound
Author: Dean R. Koontz
Category: Fiction/Thriller

Summary:
"Icebound" by Dean R. Koontz is a thrilling and chilling novel about a group of scientists who become stranded in the Arctic Circle. As they struggle to survive in the harsh environment, they soon realize they are not alone. A deadly predator is stalking them, and they must band together to fight for their lives. With twists and turns at every corner, this edge-of-your-seat thriller will keep you on the edge of your seat until the very end.
