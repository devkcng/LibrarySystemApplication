Author:  Book 1)
Category: PHILIP PULLMAN

Summary:
"The Ruby in the Smoke" is the first book in the thrilling Sally Lockhart Trilogy by Philip Pullman. Set in Victorian London, it follows the young and determined Sally Lockhart as she uncovers secrets and dangers surrounding her father's death. With the help of her friends, Sally must navigate through the treacherous world of opium traders and mysterious ruby stones to uncover the truth. Packed with adventure, suspense, and a strong female protagonist, this book is a must-read for fans of historical fiction and mystery.
