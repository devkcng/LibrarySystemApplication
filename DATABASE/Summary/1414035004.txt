Title: The Adventures of Drew and Ellie: The Magical Dress
Author: Charles Noland
Category: Children

Summary:
The Adventures of Drew and Ellie: The Magical Dress follows the story of two young siblings, Drew and Ellie, who discover a magical dress that transports them on an exciting adventure. Along the way, they encounter mythical creatures and must use their bravery and wit to overcome challenges. This charming children's book by author Charles Noland is full of imagination and heart, perfect for young readers looking for a magical journey.
