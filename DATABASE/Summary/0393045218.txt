Title: The Mummies of Urumchi
Author: E. J. W. Barber
Category: Archaeology

Summary:
The Mummies of Urumchi is a book that explores the fascinating discovery of ancient mummies in the city of Urumchi, located in the Xinjiang region of China. The author, E. J. W. Barber, delves into the history and culture of the people who lived in this region and uncovers the secrets of the well-preserved mummies and their burial practices. Through detailed archaeological research, Barber paints a vivid picture of the civilizations that thrived in this part of the world thousands of years ago. This book offers a unique glimpse into the lives of these ancient people and their customs, making it a captivating read for anyone interested in archaeology and ancient civilizations.
