ISBN: 0771074670
Title: Nights Below Station Street
Author: David Adams Richards
Category: Fiction

Summary:
"Nights Below Station Street" follows the lives of a small community in a small Canadian town as they struggle with poverty, addiction, and family dynamics. The story is told through the perspective of several characters, each facing their own personal challenges and trying to find a sense of hope and purpose in their lives. With raw and honest prose, Richards explores themes of resilience, forgiveness, and the complexities of human relationships in the face of adversity.
