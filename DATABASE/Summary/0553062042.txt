Title: Daybreakers Louis Lamour Collection
Author: Louis Lamour
Category: Fiction/Western

Summary:
The Daybreakers is a collection of short stories by renowned Western author Louis Lamour. The stories follow a group of rugged cowboys as they navigate the harsh terrain and dangerous situations of the Wild West. From cattle drives to duels, these men must use their wits and their guns to survive in a lawless land. Through thrilling action and vivid descriptions, Lamour captures the true spirit of the old American frontier. Fans of Western literature will be captivated by the tales of adventure and bravery in this classic collection.
