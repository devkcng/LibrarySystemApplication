Title: An Atmosphere of Eternity: Stories of India
Author: David Iglehart
Category: Fiction/Short Stories

Summary:
"An Atmosphere of Eternity" is a collection of short stories set in India, written by author David Iglehart. The stories explore various aspects of Indian culture, from the daily lives of its people to the rich mythology and spirituality that pervade the country. Through vivid and engaging storytelling, Iglehart captures the essence of India and its people, making for a captivating and thought-provoking read.
