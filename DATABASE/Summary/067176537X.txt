Title: The Therapeutic Touch: How to Use Your Hands to Help or to Heal
Author: Dolores Krieger
Category: Health

Summary:
"The Therapeutic Touch" teaches readers how to use their hands to provide healing and comfort to others. Written by renowned nurse and professor Dolores Krieger, this book explores the power of touch and its ability to promote relaxation, reduce pain, and improve overall well-being. With step-by-step instructions and practical tips, readers will learn how to use this gentle technique to help themselves and those around them. Whether you are a health professional or simply interested in the healing power of touch, this book is a valuable resource for anyone looking to enhance their ability to provide comfort and support.
