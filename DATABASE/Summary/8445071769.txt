Title: El Senor De Los Anillos: Las DOS Torres (Lord of the Rings (Paperback))
Author: J. R. R. Tolkien
Category: Fiction/Fantasy

Summary:
In the second installment of J. R. R. Tolkien's epic fantasy trilogy, "The Lord of the Rings," the Fellowship is broken and the fate of Middle-earth hangs in the balance. Frodo and Sam continue their journey to destroy the One Ring while Aragorn, Legolas, and Gimli search for their captured friends. Meanwhile, a new evil threatens the realm as Saruman and his army of Uruk-hai march toward the kingdom of Rohan. As the forces of good and evil converge, the fate of Middle-earth will be decided in a battle of epic proportions.
