ISBN: 0345417623
Title: Timeline
Author: MICHAEL CRICHTON
Category: Fiction/Science Fiction

Summary:
In the year 1999, a team of archaeologists and historians travel back in time to 1357 France to rescue their professor who has accidentally been transported there. As they struggle to survive in the past and find a way back to their own time, they encounter dangers and challenges they never expected. With the help of a local knight, they must navigate treacherous political alliances and a brutal war while trying to unravel the mystery of their professor's disappearance. But as they race against time, they realize that changing the past could have disastrous consequences for the future.
