ISBN: 042518630X
Title: Purity in Death
Author: J.D. Robb
Category: Mystery

Summary:
In the latest installment of the In Death series, Lieutenant Eve Dallas investigates a string of murders involving seemingly perfect victims. As she delves deeper into the case, she discovers that these victims all have one thing in common - a dark secret that may have led to their deaths. With the help of her husband Roarke and her team, Eve must race against time to stop the killer before they strike again. But as the body count rises, Eve realizes that the pursuit of purity may be the motive behind these brutal crimes.
