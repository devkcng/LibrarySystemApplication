Title: Tage der Unschuld.
Author: Richard North Patterson
Category: Fiction

Summary:
"Tage der Unschuld" is a gripping novel by Richard North Patterson that follows the story of a small town lawyer, who becomes embroiled in a high-profile murder case. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers shocking secrets and betrayal, forcing him to question the innocence of his client and the true motives of those involved. Torn between his duty as a lawyer and his moral compass, the protagonist must navigate a complex web of deceit, lies, and manipulation to uncover the truth and bring justice to the innocent.
