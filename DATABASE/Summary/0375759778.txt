Title: Prague : A Novel
Author: ARTHUR PHILLIPS
Category: Fiction

Summary:
Prague is a novel that follows the lives of five expatriates living in Budapest in the early 1990s. The story centers around the complicated relationships between the characters, their search for identity and purpose, and their struggles with love and ambition. As they navigate the vibrant city of Prague, they are forced to confront their pasts and make difficult decisions that will shape their futures. With richly developed characters and a backdrop of political and cultural change, Prague is a compelling and thought-provoking novel that explores the complexities of human nature.
