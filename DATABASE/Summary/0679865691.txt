Title: Haveli (Laurel Leaf Books)
Author: SUZANNE FISHER STAPLES
Category: Fiction

Summary:
Haveli is a captivating fiction novel by Suzanne Fisher Staples, set in India. It follows the story of a young girl named Shabanu who is forced to leave her desert home and marry an older man. As she adjusts to her new life in the haveli (traditional mansion), she uncovers secrets and challenges that threaten her happiness and safety. Shabanu must navigate the complexities of family, tradition, and love in a world filled with danger and oppression. This compelling tale explores themes of courage, resilience, and the power of love in the face of adversity.
