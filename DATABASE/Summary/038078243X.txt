Title: Miss Zukas and the Raven's Dance
Author: Jo Dereske
Category: Mystery

Summary:
In "Miss Zukas and the Raven's Dance," librarian Helma Zukas finds herself embroiled in another mystery when a local dance instructor is found dead. As she investigates, she discovers a web of secrets and lies within the small town of Bellehaven. With the help of her friend Ruth, Miss Zukas must navigate through the town's gossip and uncover the truth before the killer strikes again. Along the way, she also learns more about herself and her relationships with those around her. Will Miss Zukas be able to solve the case and bring justice to Bellehaven?
