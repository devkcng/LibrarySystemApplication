Title: Pigs in Heaven
Author: Barbara Kingsolver
Category: Fiction

Summary:
Pigs in Heaven is a heartwarming and thought-provoking novel by Barbara Kingsolver that follows the story of a Cherokee Nation woman named Taylor and her adopted daughter Turtle, as they navigate the complexities of family, identity, and cultural heritage. When a chance encounter brings Turtle's biological family into their lives, Taylor must confront difficult decisions about Turtle's future and her own relationship with her daughter. With vivid characters and beautiful prose, Pigs in Heaven explores themes of love, belonging, and the power of family bonds.
