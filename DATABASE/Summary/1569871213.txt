Title: Black Beauty (Illustrated Classics)
Author: Anna Sewell
Category: Children

Summary:
"Black Beauty" is a classic children's novel written by Anna Sewell. It tells the story of a horse named Black Beauty, who narrates his own journey from a young foal to an elegant carriage horse. Along the way, he experiences both kindness and cruelty from different owners, and learns the importance of empathy and compassion. This edition of the book is illustrated with beautiful drawings, making it a perfect introduction to a timeless and heartwarming tale.
