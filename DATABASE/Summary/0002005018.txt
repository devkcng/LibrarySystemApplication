ISBN: 0002005018
Title: Clara Callan
Author: Richard Bruce Wright
Category: Fiction

Summary:
Clara Callan tells the story of two sisters, Clara and Nora, living in rural Ontario during the 1930s. Through their letters and diary entries, we see the stark differences between their lives - Clara living in the city pursuing a career in radio, and Nora struggling to make a life for herself in the small town. As the sisters navigate through love, loss, and the changing world around them, their bond is tested and strengthened. A poignant and beautifully written novel about family, identity, and the complexities of relationships.
