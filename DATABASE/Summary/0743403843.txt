ISBN: 0743403843
Title: Decipher
Author: Stel Pavlou
Category: Science Fiction

Summary:
In the year 2045, a team of archaeologists discover a mysterious artifact that holds the key to unlocking the secrets of human existence. As they delve deeper into its origins, they uncover a sinister plot by a powerful organization to use the artifact for their own nefarious purposes. With the help of a brilliant young linguist, they must decipher the ancient symbols and prevent a catastrophic event that could change the course of humanity forever. But as they race against time, they realize that the true meaning of the artifact may be more shocking than they ever imagined.
