Title: Midnight in the Garden of Good and Evil: A Savannah Story
Author: John Berendt
Category: True Crime

Summary:
"Midnight in the Garden of Good and Evil" is a captivating true crime story that takes place in the charming city of Savannah, Georgia. The novel follows the murder trial of a wealthy antiques dealer and the eccentric characters involved in the case. With its blend of Southern charm, scandal, and mystery, this book is a page-turner that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.
