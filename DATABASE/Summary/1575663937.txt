Title: More Cunning Than Man: A Social History of Rats and Man
Author: Robert Hendrickson
Category: History

Summary:
More Cunning Than Man is a fascinating social history that explores the relationship between rats and humans. From ancient times to the present, it delves into the ways in which rats have impacted human society, from their role in plagues and diseases to their impressive adaptability and intelligence. This book offers a comprehensive look at the often overlooked but significant presence of rats in our world.
