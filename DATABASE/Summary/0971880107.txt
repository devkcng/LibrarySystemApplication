ISBN: 0971880107
Title: Wild Animus
Author: Rich Shapero
Category: Fiction

Summary:
Wild Animus is a gripping novel that follows the journey of university student Ransom Altman as he embarks on a quest to find the elusive "wild animus." Through a series of adventures and encounters, Ransom is forced to confront his own inner demons and grapple with the complexities of love and nature. With vivid imagery and thought-provoking themes, this book is a must-read for anyone seeking a thought-provoking and immersive reading experience.
