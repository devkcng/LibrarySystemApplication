Title: Tell Me This Isn't Happening
Author: Robynn Clairday
Category: Fiction

Summary:
"Tell Me This Isn't Happening" is a compelling fiction novel by author Robynn Clairday. The story follows the life of protagonist, Sarah, as she navigates through the challenges of growing up in a broken family and a tumultuous relationship. When her father suddenly disappears, leaving behind a trail of secrets, Sarah must confront her past and come to terms with the truth. Along the way, she discovers unexpected allies and learns to find strength in herself. With themes of love, loss, and resilience, this novel will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.
