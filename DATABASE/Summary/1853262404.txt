Title: Heart of Darkness (Wordsworth Collection)
Author: Joseph Conrad
Category: Fiction

Summary:
Heart of Darkness is a classic novel by Joseph Conrad that follows the journey of a man named Marlow as he travels up the Congo River in Africa. Along the way, he encounters the brutal and corrupt colonialism of the Belgian Congo and the dark heart of human nature. Through vivid descriptions and haunting themes, Conrad explores the destructive effects of imperialism and the blurred lines between civilization and savagery.
