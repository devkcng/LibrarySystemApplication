ISBN: 0312970242
Title: The Angel Is Near
Author: Deepak Chopra
Category: Spirituality

Summary:
The Angel Is Near is a thought-provoking and inspirational book written by renowned spiritual teacher Deepak Chopra. In this book, Chopra explores the concept of angels and their role in our lives, offering insights and wisdom on how we can connect with these divine beings and tap into their guidance and protection. Through personal anecdotes and spiritual teachings, Chopra invites readers to open their hearts and minds to the presence of angels and discover the transformative power of their love. The Angel Is Near is a must-read for anyone seeking a deeper understanding of spirituality and the unseen forces that guide us on our journey.
