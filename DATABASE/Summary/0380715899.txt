Title: A Soldier of the Great War
Author: Mark Helprin
Category: Fiction

Summary:
"A Soldier of the Great War" follows the journey of Alessandro Giuliani, an aging Italian professor who is drafted into World War I. As he fights for his country and witnesses the horrors of war, he reflects on his past and the beauty of life. Along the way, he befriends an unlikely companion, a young American soldier, and they embark on a harrowing and transformative journey together. Through vivid and poetic prose, Mark Helprin weaves a tale of love, loss, and the human spirit in the midst of one of the most devastating conflicts in history.
