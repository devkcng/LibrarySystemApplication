ISBN: 0425163091
Title: Chocolate Jesus
Author: Stephan Jaramillo
Category: Fiction

Summary:
"Chocolate Jesus" follows the story of a young man named Stephan who discovers that he has the ability to turn himself into a living, breathing chocolate statue of Jesus. As he struggles to cope with this newfound power and its implications, Stephan is faced with challenges and decisions that test his faith, morality, and relationships. With a touch of magical realism and a strong exploration of identity and belief, this novel delves into the complexities of faith, family, and society.
