Title: Isle of Dogs
Author: Patricia Cornwell
Category: Mystery

Summary:
In Patricia Cornwell's "Isle of Dogs," medical examiner Kay Scarpetta investigates a series of brutal murders on the Isle of Dogs in London. As she delves deeper into the case, she realizes that the killer may be targeting her and those closest to her. With the help of her team, Scarpetta must race against time to catch the killer before they strike again. With twists and turns at every corner, this gripping mystery will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.
