ISBN: 0060177586
Title: Standing Firm: A Vice-Presidential Memoir
Author: Dan Quayle
Category: Non-Fiction/Memoir

Summary:
Former Vice-President Dan Quayle reflects on his time in office and shares personal anecdotes and insights into the political landscape of the United States during the 1980s and 1990s. He also discusses his relationships with other political figures and the challenges he faced during his tenure as Vice-President.
