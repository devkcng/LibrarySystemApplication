ISBN: 0060938412
Title: The Accidental Virgin
Author: Valerie Frankel
Category: Fiction

Summary:
The Accidental Virgin is a humorous and heartfelt novel about a young woman named Katie who is determined to lose her virginity before her 30th birthday. However, her plans are thwarted when she meets a charming man who may just be worth waiting for. As she navigates the ups and downs of dating and relationships, Katie learns valuable lessons about love, self-discovery, and the meaning of true intimacy. With a relatable protagonist and witty dialogue, this book is a delightful read for anyone who has ever felt pressure to conform to societal expectations.
