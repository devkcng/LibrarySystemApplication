Title: Where You'll Find Me: And Other Stories
Author: Ann Beattie
Category: Fiction

Summary:
"Where You'll Find Me: And Other Stories" is a collection of short stories by Ann Beattie that offers glimpses into the lives of various characters dealing with issues such as love, loss, and identity. From a woman struggling with the aftermath of a failed marriage to a young girl navigating her parents' divorce, these stories explore the complexities of human relationships and the search for connection and meaning in life. With Beattie's signature wit and keen observations, this book is a poignant and thought-provoking read.
