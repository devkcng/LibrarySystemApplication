Title: Mary-Kate & Ashley Switching Goals (Mary-Kate and Ashley Starring in)
Author: Mary-Kate & Ashley Olsen
Category: Children

Summary:
In "Mary-Kate & Ashley Switching Goals," the famous twins find themselves on opposing sides of a soccer match, causing them to switch places in order to help their team win the championship. Along the way, they learn valuable lessons about teamwork and the importance of being true to oneself. This delightful children's book is filled with humor, adventure, and sisterly love, making it a must-read for fans of the Olsen twins.
