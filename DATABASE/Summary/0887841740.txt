Title: The Middle Stories
Author: Sheila Heti
Category: Fiction

Summary:
The Middle Stories is a collection of short fiction by Sheila Heti. The stories explore themes of love, relationships, and identity, often through unconventional and surreal narratives. The collection is known for its experimental style and unique blend of humor and melancholy. Heti's writing is both thought-provoking and emotionally resonant, making The Middle Stories a captivating read for fans of literary fiction.
