Title: What If?: The World's Foremost Military Historians Imagine What Might Have Been
Author: Robert Cowley
Category: History

Summary:
"What If?" is a collection of essays written by some of the world's leading military historians, exploring alternate scenarios and outcomes of major historical events. From the Battle of Gettysburg to the Cold War, these historians use their expertise and imagination to delve into the possibilities of what could have happened if certain key events had gone differently. With thought-provoking and engaging writing, this book offers a unique perspective on history and challenges readers to consider the impact of small changes on the course of world events.
