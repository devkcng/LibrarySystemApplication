Title: The Prince
Author: Niccolo Machiavelli
Category: Philosophy

Summary:
The Prince is a classic philosophical work written by Niccolo Machiavelli. It offers advice on how a ruler should gain and maintain power, emphasizing the importance of being ruthless and shrewd in political affairs. Machiavelli's ideas have been controversial and influential throughout history, making this book a must-read for anyone interested in politics and leadership.
