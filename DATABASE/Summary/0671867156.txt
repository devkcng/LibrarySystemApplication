Summary:
In "Pretend You Don't See Her" by Mary Higgins Clark, Lacey Farrell is a successful real estate agent whose life is turned upside down when she witnesses a murder. As she becomes a key witness in the case, she is forced to go into hiding and assume a new identity. But when she starts receiving threatening messages, she realizes that the killer may still be on her trail. With the help of a private investigator, Lacey must uncover the truth and stop the killer before it's too late.
