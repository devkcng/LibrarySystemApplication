Title: Under the Black Flag: The Romance and the Reality of Life Among the Pirates
Author: David Cordingly
Category: History

Summary:
In "Under the Black Flag," author David Cordingly delves into the world of pirates, exploring both the romanticized image and the harsh realities of their lives. Drawing on historical accounts and anecdotes, Cordingly paints a vivid and nuanced picture of the pirate lifestyle, covering everything from their infamous leaders to their codes of conduct. This book offers a captivating and informative read for anyone interested in the history of piracy.
