Title: The Street Lawyer
Author: JOHN GRISHAM
Category: Fiction

Summary:
Michael Brock is a successful lawyer at a prestigious firm in Washington D.C. When he witnesses a homeless man being shot outside his office, he starts to question his life and career. He leaves his high-paying job and joins a small, struggling legal clinic that helps the homeless. As he delves deeper into the world of poverty and injustice, he realizes the true meaning of being a lawyer and fights against a powerful corporation that is responsible for the shooting. Along the way, he also discovers the painful truth about his own past and confronts his own privilege and prejudices.
