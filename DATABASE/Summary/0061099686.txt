ISBN: 0061099686
Title: Downtown
Author: Anne Rivers Siddons
Category: Fiction

Summary:
"Downtown" follows the story of Caroline Venable, a young woman from a wealthy family in Charleston who rebels against her traditional upbringing and moves to New York City to pursue her dream of becoming a writer. As she navigates the bustling streets and fast-paced lifestyle of the city, Caroline faces challenges and discovers secrets about her family's past. Along the way, she forms unexpected friendships and finds love, but also must confront the harsh reality of urban life and the sacrifices it demands. This coming-of-age novel explores themes of identity, family, and finding one's place in the world.
