ISBN: 0375509038
Title: The Right Man : The Surprise Presidency of George W. Bush
Author: DAVID FRUM
Category: Political

Summary:
This book delves into the unexpected rise of George W. Bush to the presidency and examines the events and decisions that shaped his time in office. From his early political career to the controversial 2000 election and the aftermath of 9/11, author David Frum provides a detailed and insightful look at the surprise presidency of George W. Bush.
