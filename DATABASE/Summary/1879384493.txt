Title: If I'd Known Then What I Know Now: Why Not Learn from the Mistakes of Others? : You Can't Afford to Make Them All Yourself
Author: J. R. Parrish
Category: Self-Help

Summary:
This book offers valuable lessons and advice from the author's own mistakes and experiences, encouraging readers to learn from them and avoid making the same mistakes themselves. It emphasizes the importance of being open-minded and continuously learning in order to avoid costly errors in life.
