Title: Fast Women
Author: Jennifer Crusie
Category: Romance

Summary:
"Fast Women" follows the story of successful businesswoman Nell Dysart, who is trying to rebuild her life after a messy divorce. When she starts working at a private investigation agency, she meets two other strong and independent women, Stella and Mae, who become her friends and colleagues. As Nell navigates the complicated world of love and relationships, she finds herself caught up in a scandal involving her ex-husband and his mistress. With the help of her new friends and a charming new love interest, Nell must use her wit and determination to uncover the truth and find her own happily ever after.
