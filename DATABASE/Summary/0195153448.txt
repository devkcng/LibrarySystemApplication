Title: Classical Mythology
Author: Mark P. O. Morford
Category: Mythology

Summary:
"Classical Mythology" is a comprehensive and accessible introduction to the ancient myths of Greece and Rome. Written by Mark P. O. Morford, a renowned scholar of classical studies, this book covers the major gods and goddesses, heroes, and other mythical figures of the ancient world. It also delves into the origins and meanings of these stories, providing context and analysis to help readers understand their enduring relevance. With its engaging writing style and rich illustrations, "Classical Mythology" is an essential resource for anyone interested in the fascinating world of classical mythology.
