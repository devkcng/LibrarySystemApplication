Title: Proxies
Author: Laura J. Mixon
Category: Fiction

Summary:
In Laura J. Mixon's novel "Proxies," a group of humans living on a distant planet must rely on genetically engineered beings called proxies to survive. But when a mysterious illness begins to affect both humans and proxies, tensions rise and the true purpose of the proxies is called into question. As the two groups struggle to find a cure and maintain their fragile coexistence, the lines between creator and creation blur, forcing them to confront the consequences of their actions.
