Title: Getting Well Again
Author: O. Carol Simonton Md
Category: Health

Summary:
"Getting Well Again" is a comprehensive guide to the mind-body connection in healing, written by renowned psychiatrist and cancer researcher Dr. O. Carol Simonton. Using case studies and scientific evidence, Dr. Simonton explains how the power of the mind can influence the body's ability to fight disease and promote healing. She also offers practical techniques for managing stress, developing a positive mindset, and creating a supportive environment for recovery. This book is a valuable resource for anyone seeking to take an active role in their own healing process.
