ISBN: 0399135782
Title: The Kitchen God's Wife
Author: Amy Tan
Category: Fiction

Summary:
The Kitchen God's Wife is a powerful and emotional novel by Amy Tan that explores the complex relationships between mothers and daughters, as well as the struggles of Chinese immigrant families in America. Through the story of a Chinese-American woman and her mother, the novel delves into themes of love, sacrifice, and the immigrant experience. With richly developed characters and a compelling plot, The Kitchen God's Wife is a moving and thought-provoking read.
