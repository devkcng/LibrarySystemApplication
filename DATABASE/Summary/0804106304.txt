ISBN: 0804106304
Title: The Joy Luck Club
Author: Amy Tan
Category: Fiction

Summary:
The Joy Luck Club follows the lives of four Chinese-American women and their relationships with their immigrant mothers. Through a series of interwoven stories, the novel explores themes of cultural identity, mother-daughter bonds, and the struggle to find a balance between tradition and modernity. As the women share their pasts and present struggles, they come to understand and appreciate each other's experiences, ultimately finding strength and resilience in their shared heritage.
