Title: Goodbye to the Buttermilk Sky
Author: Julia Oliver
Category: Fiction

Summary:
Goodbye to the Buttermilk Sky is a novel by Julia Oliver about a young woman named Sky who must say goodbye to her idyllic childhood and face the harsh realities of adulthood. Set in the 1950s, the novel follows Sky as she navigates love, loss, and the changing social landscape of the time. As she says goodbye to the simplicity of her childhood, she learns valuable lessons about life, love, and the importance of family. With beautiful prose and vivid imagery, this novel captures the essence of a bygone era and the universal struggles of growing up.
