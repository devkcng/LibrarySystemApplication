Title: Lying Awake
Author: Mark Salzman
Category: Fiction

Summary:
Lying Awake tells the story of Sister John of the Cross, a cloistered nun who begins to experience mystical visions that inspire her poetry. As she struggles to reconcile her spiritual experiences with her strict religious community, Sister John faces a difficult decision that will test her faith and her identity as a nun. With beautiful prose and deep insights into the human condition, Mark Salzman's novel explores the complexities of faith and the search for meaning in a modern world.
