Title: New Vegetarian: Bold and Beautiful Recipes for Every Occasion
Author: Celia Brooks Brown
Category: Cookbook

Summary:
"The New Vegetarian" offers bold and beautiful recipes for every occasion, written by renowned vegetarian chef Celia Brooks Brown. With a focus on fresh, seasonal ingredients and creative flavor combinations, this cookbook is perfect for both vegetarians and those looking to incorporate more plant-based meals into their diet. From hearty mains to delicious sides and desserts, this book has something for every taste and occasion.
