Title: Tess of the D'Urbervilles (Wordsworth Classics)
Author: Thomas Hardy
Category: Fiction

Summary:
In rural England, a young woman named Tess Durbeyfield is sent to claim kinship with the wealthy D'Urberville family. She is seduced and impregnated by the manipulative Alec D'Urberville, leading to tragic consequences for Tess and those around her. This classic novel by Thomas Hardy explores themes of social class, morality, and the role of fate in one's life.
