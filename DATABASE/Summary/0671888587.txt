ISBN: 0671888587
Title: I'll Be Seeing You
Author: Mary Higgins Clark
Category: Fiction/Mystery

Summary:
In this thrilling mystery, a young woman named Kay Lansing inherits her grandmother's estate and discovers a shocking secret that connects her to a notorious murder case from the past. As she delves deeper into the mystery, she realizes she may be in grave danger. With the help of her former flame, private investigator Lenny Brookman, Kay must unravel the truth before it's too late. From the streets of New York City to the tranquil countryside of Connecticut, Kay and Lenny race against time to uncover a sinister plot that threatens their lives.
