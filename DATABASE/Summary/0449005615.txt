ISBN: 0449005615
Title: Seabiscuit: An American Legend
Author: LAURA HILLENBRAND
Category: Non-Fiction

Summary:
Seabiscuit: An American Legend is a non-fiction book by Laura Hillenbrand that tells the story of the famous racehorse Seabiscuit. The book follows the horse's rise to fame and his impact on the world of horse racing during the 1930s. Through Hillenbrand's extensive research and vivid storytelling, readers are taken on a journey of triumph, tragedy, and the enduring spirit of this beloved American legend.
