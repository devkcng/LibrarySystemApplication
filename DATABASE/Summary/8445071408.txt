Summary:
"El Senor De Los Anillos: LA Comunidad Del Anillo" is the first book in J.R.R. Tolkien's epic fantasy series, "Lord of the Rings." The story follows a hobbit named Frodo Baggins, who inherits a powerful ring from his uncle. Realizing the danger it poses, Frodo sets out on a journey to destroy the ring and stop the evil Sauron from gaining control of Middle-earth. Along the way, Frodo is joined by a group of companions, forming the Fellowship of the Ring.
