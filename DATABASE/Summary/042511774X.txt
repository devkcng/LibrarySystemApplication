Title: Breathing Lessons
Author: Anne Tyler
Category: Fiction

Summary:
Breathing Lessons by Anne Tyler is a heartfelt and humorous novel that follows the story of a couple, Maggie and Ira, on a road trip to a friend's funeral. As they navigate the ups and downs of their 28-year marriage, they reflect on the past, their children, and the choices they've made. Along the way, they encounter a series of eccentric characters and unexpected detours that force them to confront their own fears and desires. Through it all, they learn to appreciate the beauty and complexity of their relationship, and the power of love to endure.
