Title: Sturmzeit. Roman.
Author: Charlotte Link
Category: Fiction

Summary:
"Sturmzeit" is a gripping novel by Charlotte Link that follows the lives of two women, Henny and Käthe, during the tumultuous years of World War II. As the war ravages Germany and changes the lives of everyone around them, Henny and Käthe must navigate the challenges and dangers of the time. With richly detailed characters and a compelling plot, this novel explores the complexities of love, friendship, and survival in a time of chaos and uncertainty.
