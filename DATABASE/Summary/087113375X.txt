Title: Modern Manners: An Etiquette Book for Rude People
Author: P.J. O'Rourke
Category: Etiquette

Summary:
"Modern Manners" is a humorous and irreverent guide to etiquette for those who may not have the best manners. Written by P.J. O'Rourke, a well-known political satirist, this book offers a witty take on traditional etiquette rules and provides practical advice for navigating social situations with tact and grace. With a tongue-in-cheek approach, O'Rourke tackles topics such as dining etiquette, party behavior, and workplace interactions, making this an entertaining read for both the etiquette-challenged and those looking for a good laugh.
