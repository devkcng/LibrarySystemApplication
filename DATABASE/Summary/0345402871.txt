ISBN: 0345402871
Title: Airframe
Author: Michael Crichton
Category: Fiction/Thriller

Summary:
Airframe is a fast-paced thriller that follows the investigation of a mysterious plane crash that leaves three people dead. The main character, Casey Singleton, is a quality assurance expert for a major airline who is tasked with uncovering the cause of the crash. As she delves deeper, she discovers a web of corporate greed, cover-ups, and sabotage. With the help of a team of experts and her own intuition, Casey races against time to find the truth and prevent another disaster. As the investigation progresses, she finds herself in danger as powerful forces try to stop her from uncovering the truth. A gripping and suspenseful read, Airframe will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.
