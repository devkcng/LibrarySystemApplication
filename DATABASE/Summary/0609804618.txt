Title: Our Dumb Century: The Onion Presents 100 Years of Headlines from America's Finest News Source
Author: The Onion

Summary:
"Our Dumb Century" is a hilarious collection of headlines from "America's Finest News Source," The Onion, spanning 100 years of satirical news coverage. From historical events to pop culture trends, this book offers a unique and absurd perspective on American history.
