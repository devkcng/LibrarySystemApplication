ISBN: 0156047624
Title: All the King's Men
Author: Robert Penn Warren
Category: Fiction

Summary:
"All the King's Men" follows the rise and fall of Willie Stark, a charismatic and ambitious politician in the American South. As he gains power and influence, his moral compass begins to blur and his relationships with those around him become increasingly complicated. Through the eyes of Jack Burden, Stark's right-hand man, the novel explores themes of corruption, ambition, and the consequences of pursuing power at any cost. A Pulitzer Prize-winning classic, "All the King's Men" is a poignant and insightful examination of the human condition.
