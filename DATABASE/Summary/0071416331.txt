Title: Team Bush : Leadership Lessons from the Bush White House
Author: Donald F. Kettl
Category: Leadership

Summary:
"Team Bush: Leadership Lessons from the Bush White House" is a book that explores the leadership style of former President George W. Bush and his team. Written by Donald F. Kettl, a renowned expert in public policy and governance, the book delves into the decisions and strategies implemented by the Bush administration, and how they can be applied to leadership roles in any organization. Through real-life examples and interviews with key members of the Bush team, Kettl provides valuable insights on effective leadership, decision-making, and crisis management. This book is a must-read for anyone looking to learn from the successes and failures of the Bush administration and apply them in their own leadership roles.
