ISBN: 0394743741
Title: The yawning heights
Author: Aleksandr Zinoviev
Category: Fiction

Summary:
"The Yawning Heights" is a satirical novel set in Soviet Russia, following the lives of a group of intellectuals as they navigate the absurdities and contradictions of the communist regime. Through dark humor and biting wit, author Aleksandr Zinoviev critiques the oppressive nature of the Soviet system and the hypocrisy of its leaders. As the characters struggle to maintain their ideals and sanity in a society built on lies and propaganda, they are forced to confront their own hopes and fears in a world that seems to be constantly yawning towards destruction.
