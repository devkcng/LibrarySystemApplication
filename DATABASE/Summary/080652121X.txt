Title: Hitler's Secret Bankers: The Myth of Swiss Neutrality During the Holocaust
Author: Adam Lebor
Category: History

Summary:
In "Hitler's Secret Bankers," author Adam Lebor challenges the widely accepted notion of Swiss neutrality during the Holocaust. He delves into the role of Swiss banks in financing Nazi Germany and aiding in the looting of Jewish assets. Lebor reveals the complicity of Swiss bankers in perpetuating the horrors of the Holocaust, and sheds light on the long-standing myth of Swiss neutrality. This thought-provoking book sheds new light on a dark chapter in history and raises important questions about the role of neutral countries in times of war.
