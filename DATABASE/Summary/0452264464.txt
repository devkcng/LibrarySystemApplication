Title: Beloved (Plume Contemporary Fiction)
Author: Toni Morrison
Category: Fiction

Summary:
Beloved is a haunting and powerful novel that explores the lasting effects of slavery and its impact on the human psyche. The story follows Sethe, a former slave who is haunted by the memory of her daughter, Beloved, who died tragically. When a mysterious young woman arrives at her doorstep claiming to be her reincarnated daughter, Sethe must confront the trauma of her past and the difficult choices she made to protect her family. With lyrical prose and emotional depth, Toni Morrison weaves a gripping tale of love, loss, and the enduring strength of the human spirit.
