ISBN: 0842342702
Title: Left Behind: A Novel of the Earth's Last Days (Left Behind #1)
Author: Tim Lahaye
Category: Fiction/Religious

Summary:
"Left Behind" is an action-packed novel that follows the lives of a group of people who are left behind on Earth after the Rapture, a biblical event where believers are taken to heaven. As they struggle to make sense of the chaos and destruction that ensues, they must also confront the truth of the end times and the ultimate battle between good and evil. This gripping story weaves together elements of faith, suspense, and relationships as the characters navigate a world that is rapidly approaching its final days.
