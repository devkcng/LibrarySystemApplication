Title: Seabiscuit
Author: LAURA HILLENBRAND
Category: Non-Fiction/Sports

Summary:
"Seabiscuit" by Laura Hillenbrand is a non-fiction book that tells the incredible story of a small, underestimated racehorse who became a symbol of hope and resilience during the Great Depression. Set in the world of horse racing, the book follows Seabiscuit's journey from his humble beginnings to becoming one of the most celebrated and beloved racehorses of all time. Through the perspectives of the horse's owner, trainer, and jockey, Hillenbrand captures the determination and spirit of Seabiscuit and the people who believed in him. This inspiring and heartwarming story of an unlikely champion is a must-read for sports fans and non-fiction lovers alike.
