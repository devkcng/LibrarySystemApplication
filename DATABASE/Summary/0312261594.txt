ISBN: 0312261594
Title: Female Intelligence
Author: Jane Heller
Category: Fiction

Summary:
"Female Intelligence" is a humorous and clever novel that follows the story of Lynn Wyman, a successful and independent woman who finds herself caught in a web of lies and espionage when she is recruited by the CIA to pose as a spy. As she navigates through the dangerous world of espionage, Lynn must also confront her own personal struggles and insecurities, leading to unexpected twists and turns. With sharp wit and relatable characters, this book is a must-read for fans of clever and entertaining fiction.
