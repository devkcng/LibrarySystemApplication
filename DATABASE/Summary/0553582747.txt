Title: From the Corner of His Eye
Author: Dean Koontz
Category: Fiction/Thriller

Summary:
"From the Corner of His Eye" is a gripping thriller by bestselling author Dean Koontz. It follows the story of Barty Lampion, a young boy with extraordinary gifts, and Junior Cain, a ruthless killer who becomes obsessed with Barty. As the two cross paths, a thrilling cat and mouse game ensues, filled with twists and turns that will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end. With supernatural elements and heart-stopping action, this novel is a must-read for fans of suspense and mystery.
