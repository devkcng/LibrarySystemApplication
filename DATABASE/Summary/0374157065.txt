Title: Flu: The Story of the Great Influenza Pandemic of 1918 and the Search for the Virus That Caused It
Author: Gina Bari Kolata
Category: History/Science

Summary:
In 1918, a deadly influenza pandemic swept the world, killing millions of people. In "Flu," Gina Bari Kolata tells the story of this devastating outbreak and the scientific efforts to understand and combat it. From the initial confusion and fear to the ongoing search for the virus responsible, this book provides a gripping account of one of the worst pandemics in human history.
