ISBN: 0440234743
Title: The Testament
Author: John Grisham
Category: Fiction

Summary:
The Testament is a legal thriller following the story of billionaire Troy Phelan, who leaves behind a shocking will that disinherits his six children and instead leaves his entire fortune to an unknown illegitimate daughter living in the Brazilian rainforest. The novel follows the legal battle over the will as well as the journey of the young woman as she inherits her newfound wealth and learns about her father's past. Full of twists and turns, The Testament is a gripping page-turner that delves into themes of family, greed, and redemption.
