Title: Decision in Normandy
Author: Carlo D'Este
Category: History/Military

Summary:
Decision in Normandy is a comprehensive and gripping account of the Allied invasion of Normandy during World War II. Written by acclaimed military historian Carlo D'Este, the book provides a detailed and well-researched analysis of the strategic decisions made by the Allied commanders and the impact these decisions had on the outcome of the invasion. D'Este also delves into the personal lives and personalities of the key players, offering a unique perspective on the events that shaped one of the most important battles in modern history. With its vivid storytelling and meticulous attention to detail, Decision in Normandy is a must-read for anyone interested in military history.
