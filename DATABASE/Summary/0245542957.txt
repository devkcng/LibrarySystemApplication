ISBN: 0245542957
Title: Pacific Northwest
Author: Hans Johannes Hoefer
Category: Travel

Summary:
The Pacific Northwest is a comprehensive guide to the stunning region of the United States that includes Washington, Oregon, and parts of British Columbia. It covers everything from bustling cities to picturesque landscapes, providing readers with information on the top destinations, attractions, and activities to explore in this beautiful area. With detailed descriptions and helpful tips, this book is the perfect companion for anyone planning a trip to the Pacific Northwest.
