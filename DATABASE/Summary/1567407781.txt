Title: The Witchfinder (Amos Walker Mystery Series)
Author: Loren D. Estleman
Category: Mystery

Summary:
In "The Witchfinder," private investigator Amos Walker searches for a missing woman in Detroit's underground goth scene, only to stumble upon a dangerous cult led by a man claiming to be a modern-day witchfinder. As Walker delves deeper into the case, he must confront his own beliefs about the supernatural and face off against a powerful and ruthless enemy. With his trademark wit and cunning, Walker must use all his skills to solve this twisted mystery before it's too late.
