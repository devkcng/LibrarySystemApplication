ISBN: 0440223571
Title: This Year It Will Be Different: And Other Stories
Author: Maeve Binchy
Category: Fiction/Short Stories

Summary:
This collection of short stories by Maeve Binchy explores the theme of change and how it affects the lives of her characters. From a young couple facing their first Christmas together to a family struggling with the loss of a loved one, Binchy's stories capture the ups and downs of life with her signature warmth and wit. Each story offers a poignant and relatable glimpse into the human experience, making this book a perfect read for the holiday season.
