ISBN: 0375410538
Title: Anil's Ghost
Author: MICHAEL ONDAATJE
Category: Fiction

Summary:
Anil's Ghost is a novel by Michael Ondaatje that follows the story of Anil, a forensic anthropologist who returns to her native Sri Lanka to investigate human rights violations during the country's civil war. As she uncovers the truth behind the murders and disappearances, she is joined by a mysterious man named Sarath, and together they navigate the complexities of war, politics, and personal relationships. Through vivid prose and haunting imagery, Ondaatje explores the impact of violence and the search for identity in a war-torn nation.
