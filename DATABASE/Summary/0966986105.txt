ISBN: 0966986105
Title: Prescription for Terror
Author: Sandra Levy Ceren
Category: Fiction/Thriller

Summary:
In Prescription for Terror, Sandra Levy Ceren weaves a gripping tale of suspense and danger. The story follows psychiatrist Dr. Sarah Kagan, who becomes embroiled in a terrifying game of cat and mouse when one of her patients disappears without a trace. As she delves deeper into the case, Sarah realizes that her patient was involved in a dangerous plot involving a deadly virus and a powerful organization. With her life and the lives of those around her on the line, Sarah must race against time to uncover the truth and stop the impending threat before it's too late. Full of twists and turns, Prescription for Terror will keep readers on the edge of their seats until the very end.
