ISBN: 0671870432
Title: PLEADING GUILTY
Author: Scott Turow
Category: Fiction

Summary:
Pleading Guilty follows the story of Mack Malloy, a former lawyer turned investigator, who is tasked with finding a missing partner at his old law firm. As he delves deeper into the case, he uncovers a web of deceit and corruption within the firm. With his own past and secrets coming back to haunt him, Mack must navigate through dangerous territory to uncover the truth.
