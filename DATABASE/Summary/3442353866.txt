ISBN: 3442353866
Title: Der Fluch der Kaiserin. Ein Richter- Di- Roman.
Author: Eleanor Cooney
Category: Fiction

Summary:
The novel "Der Fluch der Kaiserin" tells the story of a judge named Di who is given a difficult case involving a cursed empress. As he delves into the mystery surrounding the empress, Di uncovers dark secrets and must navigate dangerous political intrigue in order to uncover the truth and deliver justice. Along the way, he also begins to question his own beliefs and values. This gripping novel combines elements of mystery, history, and political thriller to create a thrilling read.
