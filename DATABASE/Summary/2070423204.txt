Title: Lieux dits
Author: Michel Tournier
Category: Fiction

Summary:
"Lieux dits" by Michel Tournier is a work of fiction that explores the theme of identity through the lens of place. The novel follows the story of a man who embarks on a journey to discover the meaning behind the names of various locations in France. Through his travels, he reflects on his own sense of self and the impact of environment on one's identity. Tournier's poignant writing delves into the complexities of human existence and the power of the places we call home. This thought-provoking novel is a must-read for anyone interested in the relationship between self and surroundings.
